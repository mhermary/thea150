Fishing Story

We were assigned to tell a story between 3 and 4 minutes in length; we were reminded that stories are told, not read. We were given advice on how to connect with out audience by using eye contact, emotion, and exposing a vulnerability. We had to know the story by heart and were graded on the 7 C's
Clarity - Be clear in thoughts and delivery.
Command - Be centered and in control.
Correct - Any facts included must be true.
Concise - Get to the point with no meandering.
Coherent - Follow the falling dominos. Keep it logical.
Changed of Colour - Varied pitch and pace of delivery.
Complete - Conclude with the storied effect on you.
We were also marked on the overall effectiveness of the presentation, inclusion of a vulnerability, and analysis/critique of a classmates story.
